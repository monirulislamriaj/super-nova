Why is the company called Supernova?

One sun sustains all life on planet earth. One Supernova shines 570 billion times brighter than the sun. We believe it’s time for solo stars (Founders) to combine our life force energy and shine brighter together (Supernova). To unify our superpowers as quantum creators and play as one all-star team for venture humanity. Let’s light up the world.

What makes Supernova different?

We call it oneness at work.™ You allocate on advisory board seat to Supernova! In turn, you get access to our all-star community of exited founders and superstars. Beyond being guiding lights now orbiting around you, we benefit from our best-in-class services. We are an extension of your team to manifest your dreams in hyperspeed.

Is Supernova like a VC?

No, we are NOT a VC fund. This frees up form unicorn hunting! Instead, we focus on collaboration for real innovation. Paradigm shifts in the way we come together as a conscious community to build thriving businesses and living masterpieces. Evolving our ethos and ultimately relationships from transactional to transformational is a game-changer.
